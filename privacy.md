---

## 1 · What we may collect

| Type | Examples |
|------|-----------|
| **IDs** | Anonymous or app-related IDs for sync / optional cloud backup |
| **Usage & diagnostics** | Feature usage, crashes, performance (**Firebase Analytics**, **Crashlytics**) |
| **What you enter** | Alarms, habits, mood notes, sleep logs; optional **voice → text** on device where applicable |
| **Purchases** | Subscription **status** via Apple / Google and partners like **RevenueCat** — we don’t get your full card number |
| **Ads (Android, free tier)** | If shown, **AdMob** and partners may collect data per **their** policies |

---

## 2 · How we use it

- Run and improve the App (alarms, reminders, insights, UI)  
- Handle subscriptions and **restore purchases**  
- Power **optional AI** when **you** ask for it  
- Security, bug fixes, aggregate analytics  
- Legal compliance and enforcing our terms  

---

## 3 · AI

For AI features, text you send may go to our AI provider (e.g. **Groq**) to generate a reply. **Don’t** send data you’re uncomfortable sharing. Avoid highly sensitive health details in free text.

## 4 · Third parties (summary)

We keep data as long as needed for the App and legitimate purposes (security, law). You may delete account & data **in-app** (e.g. **Delete account & data** in Settings). Some data may be kept where **law** or disputes require it.
